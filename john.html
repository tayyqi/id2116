Hi I'm John

I joined Industrial Design because I liked to imagine how things could be different and make a change. I also joined because I really disliked my Junior College education. I hated the idea of having to take exams and be graded on what I wrote on paper rather than what I could do with that knowledge. In Industrial Design I can finally express what I imagine in my head and not have to explain everything in an essay. As a designer I wish to help people live more comfortably and enjoy their lives more through the products that I design. My design motto is, “Make things beautiful and then makes sure it works.”

4 strengths as a designer:

  • Rhinoceros, grasshopper and Rendering.
  • Receptive to my surroundings and drawing inspiration from around me.
  • I love working with people and making project work fun!
  • I listen to my group mates ideas and try to understand their ideas.
